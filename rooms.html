OUR ROOMS

Nestled in the heart of nature, Manahayu Resort is a serene retreat where tradition and tranquility come together. Inspired by rich cultural heritage and stunning landscapes, we blend authentic local traditions with the beauty of nature. With traditional architecture, handcrafted interiors, and lush surroundings, we offer a unique escape where guests can immerse themselves in culture and serenity.

Family Room

Every corner of Manahayu Resort reflects the rich cultural heritage passed down through generations. Our buildings are designed with elegant traditional architecture, using natural materials such as wood and stone to harmonize with the surrounding environment.
