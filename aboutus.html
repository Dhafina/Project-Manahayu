ABOUT US

Nestled in the heart of nature, Manahayu Resort is a serene retreat where tradition and tranquility come together. Inspired by rich cultural heritage and stunning landscapes, we blend authentic local traditions with the beauty of nature. With traditional architecture, handcrafted interiors, and lush surroundings, we offer a unique escape where guests can immerse themselves in culture and serenity.

Cultural Heritage and Authentic Experiences

Every corner of Manahayu Resort reflects the rich cultural heritage passed down through generations. Our buildings are designed with elegant traditional architecture, using natural materials such as wood and stone to harmonize with the surrounding environment. We also offer various cultural experiences, from local art performances and handicraft workshops to authentic cuisine prepared with time-honored recipes.

Our Mission
Manahayu is committed to preserving Indonesia’s traditional heritage through nature-integrated resort experiences. We offer a serene retreat where guests can reconnect with nature, culture, and themselves. By harmonizing modern comfort with local values and environmental awareness, we promote sustainable tourism. Authentic hospitality and support for local communities—through the integration of culture and craftsmanship—are at the heart of every experience we provide.
